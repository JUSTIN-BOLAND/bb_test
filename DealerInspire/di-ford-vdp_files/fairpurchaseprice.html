Definitions

Values and pricing are the opinions of Kelley Blue Book, and the actual price of a vehicle may vary. Kelley Blue Book assumes no responsibility for errors or omissions.

Kelley Blue Book® Fair Purchase Price

This Kelley Blue Book® Fair Purchase Price is based in part on the MSRP supplied by the dealer offering this vehicle for sale. Updated weekly, the Kelley Blue Book® Fair Purchase Price for new cars is generally the midpoint of the Fair Market Range. The Fair Purchase Price is Kelley Blue Book's estimate of what a consumer can reasonably expect to pay this week in this vehicle's area for a vehicle of the same year, make, model and MSRP, excluding taxes, title, fees and any available rebates or incentives. It's calculated based on the MSRP, actual new-car transactions, and data from other reliable third-party sources as well as market conditions. Each dealer sets and controls its own pricing.

Fair Market Range (Buy from a Dealer)

This Kelley Blue Book® Fair Market Range is based in part on the MSRP supplied by the dealer offering this vehicle for sale. Updated weekly the Kelley Blue Book® Fair Market Range for new cars is Kelley Blue Book's estimate of what a consumer can reasonably expect to pay this week in this vehicle's area for a vehicle of the same year, make, model and MSRP, excluding taxes, title, fees and any available rebates or incentives. Each dealer sets and controls its own pricing.

Invoice

The invoice is the price the manufacturer charges its dealers for a new vehicle. The invoice price includes destination charges, but doesn't include dealer advertising costs or costs for selling, preparing, displaying or financing the vehicle. Also, Kelley Blue Book prices include holdbacks and factory-to-dealer incentives which can lower the effective dealer cost in some cases.

MSRP

The MSRP, or Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price, shown here was supplied by the dealer offering this vehicle for sale. MSRP is the price set by the manufacturer and does not necessarily reflect the price actually paid by consumers. This MSRP excludes service and handling fees and state and local tax, title and license fees. Destination and freight charges may also be excluded.

Red Zone (Buy from a Dealer)

The red zone falls above the Fair Market Range. Special circumstances such as exceptionally low supply with high demand, options installed by the dealership, credit, and seasonality may all be contributing factors to sales that occur in this range.

White Zone (Buy from a Dealer)

The white zone falls below the Fair Market Range. Special circumstances such as retiring model years, unpopular color, excess inventory and seasonality may all be contributing factors to sales that occur in this range.

Dealer Asking Price

Dealer Asking Price is the price this dealer is asking for the car, not including rebates or incentives.

Transaction Data

This meter represents a sample range of recent transaction prices for vehicles of the same year, make and model as the vehicle specified, but sometimes with a different style and/or equipment. Analyzed transaction prices exclude taxes, DMV fees, documentation fees, other dealership charges and any available rebates or incentives.

Why Don't I See a Fair Purchase Price?

That depends.  Since Kelley Blue Book bases Fair Purchase Price on actual transactions, some vehicles are too new to establish a history, others have low current dealer inventory, and still others have low sales volume overall.

For these reasons, some additional info may be unavailable as well.
